Our Services

Everything your ground operations team needs — in one unified platform.

What We Provide

Built for Every Part of Ground Ops

Airport Ops brings together six core modules under one roof — giving your team real-time visibility and control across every aspect of airport ground operations.

✈️

Flight Tracking

Monitor live flight schedules, status updates, and delays across your entire network. Assign aircraft, update departure times, and keep every stakeholder in sync from a single dashboard.

  • Live schedule management
  • Status updates (Scheduled, Boarding, Departed, Arrived, Cancelled)
  • Aircraft and airline assignment
  • Origin and destination tracking
🧳

Baggage Handling

Track every bag from check-in to claim with a full status history. Quickly identify delayed or misrouted baggage and resolve issues before they reach the passenger.

  • End-to-end baggage status tracking
  • Flight-linked bag records
  • Delayed and misrouted bag alerts
  • Full audit trail per bag
🚪

Gate Management

Get real-time gate availability and assignment across every terminal. Prevent conflicts, optimize utilisation, and give ground crew instant visibility into gate status.

  • Live gate availability board
  • Flight-to-gate assignment
  • Terminal and concourse grouping
  • Conflict detection
👷

Staff Scheduling

Coordinate ground crew, security personnel, and maintenance teams across shifts. Manage roles, assignments, and availability — all without spreadsheets.

  • Role-based staff profiles
  • Shift and availability tracking
  • Department-wise grouping
  • Search and filter by role or name
🔧

Maintenance Logs

Log, assign, and track every aircraft maintenance request from report to resolution. Ensure no issue slips through the cracks before a flight departs.

  • Issue reporting per aircraft
  • Priority-level tagging
  • Assigned technician tracking
  • Status flow: Pending → In Progress → Resolved
📋

Operational Reports

Generate exportable reports across flights, gates, baggage, staff, and maintenance. Get the data you need to make faster operational decisions.

  • Cross-module report generation
  • Date-range filtering
  • Summary stats and breakdowns
  • Export-ready format
