Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about the Airport Ground Operations Management System. Can't find what you're looking for? Contact our support team.

🛫 General

It is a unified command platform for managing flights, baggage, gates, staff, and maintenance — giving every operator total situational awareness from a single interface in real time.

The platform is deployed at partner airports and can be configured for any size of airport — from regional hubs to international terminals. Contact your airport administrator to confirm availability at your facility.

Yes. The system is designed for continuous availability. Planned maintenance windows are communicated in advance and are typically scheduled during the lowest-traffic hours to minimise disruption.

The system works best on the latest versions of Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, and Mozilla Firefox. Safari is also supported. Internet Explorer is not supported.

🔐 Accounts & Access

Access is limited to authorised airport personnel. You may register as Ground Staff, Supervisor, or Maintenance crew. All new accounts are reviewed and activated by a Supervisor or Admin before access is granted.

New registrations require approval from a Supervisor or Admin. This typically takes up to one business day. You will receive an email notification once your account is activated. If it has been longer than 24 hours, contact your supervisor directly.

Click the "Forgot password?" link on the Sign In page. Enter your registered email address and a password reset link will be sent to you. The link is valid for 30 minutes. If you don't receive the email, check your spam folder or contact your IT helpdesk.

Role changes must be approved by a Supervisor or Admin. You cannot change your own role. Raise a request with your supervisor, who can update your role from the admin panel.

Change your password immediately using the Forgot password link, and report the incident to your supervisor and IT helpdesk at support@airportops.in. Do not share your credentials with anyone.

⚙️ Operations

The platform includes six core modules: Flight Management, Gate Management, Baggage Tracking, Staff Scheduling, Maintenance Requests, and Reports & Analytics. Access to each module depends on your assigned role.

Flight data is updated in real time via live sync with connected data sources. The Live Flight Board on the dashboard refreshes automatically — no manual page reload is needed.

Yes. Reports can be exported in CSV and PDF formats from the Reports & Analytics module. Supervisors and Admins can also schedule automated reports to be delivered by email.

Navigate to the Maintenance module from the dashboard sidebar, click "New Request", fill in the details (location, category, urgency), and submit. Your supervisor and the maintenance team will be notified automatically.

🛠 Technical & Support

Try a hard refresh (Ctrl + Shift + R on Windows, Cmd + Shift + R on Mac). If the issue persists, clear your browser cache, try a different browser, or check your internet connection. If none of that helps, contact your IT helpdesk.

Email support@airportops.in or call your airport IT helpdesk. For urgent operational issues, contact your supervisor directly. Support hours are 24/7 for critical incidents.

The platform is fully responsive and works on mobile browsers. A dedicated native mobile app is on the roadmap. For now, add the web app to your home screen from your mobile browser for a near-native experience.
