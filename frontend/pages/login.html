✈ Frequently Asked Questions

What is Airport Ground Operations Management System?

It is a unified command platform for managing flights, baggage, gates, staff, and maintenance — giving every operator total situational awareness from a single interface.

Who can register?

Access is limited to authorised airport personnel. You may register as Ground Staff, Supervisor, or Maintenance crew. New accounts are reviewed before activation.

I forgot my password. What do I do?

Click the "Forgot password?" link on the Sign In tab. A reset link will be sent to your registered email address.

Why is my account not activated yet?

New registrations require approval from a Supervisor or Admin. You will receive an email once your account is activated.

Which browsers are supported?

The system works best on the latest versions of Chrome, Edge, and Firefox. Internet Explorer is not supported.

Who do I contact for technical support?

Reach out to your airport IT helpdesk or email support@airportops.in.

🔒 Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2024

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide during registration (username, email, role, phone) and activity data generated while using the platform (login timestamps, actions performed).

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate and authorise your access to the system.
  • To provide operational features relevant to your role.
  • To generate audit logs for safety and compliance purposes.
  • To send system notifications and alerts.

Data Sharing

Your data is not sold or shared with third parties. It may be shared with authorised supervisors and administrators within your airport organisation for operational needs.

Data Retention

Account data is retained for the duration of your employment. Audit logs may be retained for up to 7 years as required by aviation safety regulations.

Security

We use industry-standard encryption and access controls to protect your data. Passwords are stored using secure hashing algorithms.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data by contacting your system administrator.
