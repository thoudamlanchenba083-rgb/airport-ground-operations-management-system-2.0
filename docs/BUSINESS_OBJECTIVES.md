# Business Objectives

## Problem Statement
Airports coordinate aircraft handling, gate assignments, maintenance activities, baggage
operations, and staff scheduling across multiple teams in real time. Poor coordination
between these functions causes flight delays, gate conflicts, understaffed shifts, and
slow response to equipment/maintenance issues — all of which directly impact on-time
performance and passenger experience.

## Measurable Business Goals
| Goal | Metric | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Reduce gate assignment conflicts | Double-booked gate incidents / month | 0 |
| Faster turnaround visibility | Avg. time to detect turnaround delay | < 5 min (real-time) |
| Reduce missed maintenance | Overdue maintenance requests | -50% |
| Improve staff utilization | Shift coverage gaps | -30% |
| Faster incident response | Avg. time from incident log to assignment | < 10 min |
| Data-driven staffing | % of shifts planned using AI staffing prediction | 80%+ |

## Operational Improvements
- Single source of truth for flight/gate/staff/equipment status (replaces spreadsheets/radio calls)
- Automated notifications instead of manual follow-ups
- Audit trail for every operational action (accountability, compliance)
- Predictive alerts (delay risk, maintenance urgency) instead of reactive handling

## Reporting Requirements
- Daily operations summary (flights handled, delays, incidents)
- Staff utilization and shift coverage reports
- Maintenance/equipment health reports
- Gate utilization reports
- Exportable reports (PDF/CSV) for management review

## Workflow Automation Opportunities
- Auto-assign gates based on aircraft type/availability (AI Module gate recommendation)
- Auto-flag flights at delay risk (AI Module delay prediction)
- Auto-notify staff/supervisors on status changes (Notifications app)
- Auto-escalate overdue maintenance requests
